About Us

About

International Youth Activity Centre – Bulgaria

Empowering Youth Through Action and Connection

IYAC Bulgaria (International Youth Activity Center) is a dynamic non-profit youth organization, dedicated to inspiring and empowering young people through education, volunteering, and international cooperation. Since 2014, we have been creating meaningful opportunities for youth to grow personally and professionally, while becoming active citizens in their communities and beyond. Our work spans local and international initiatives, from organizing Erasmus+ youth exchanges and training courses to leading cultural and educational projects across Europe. Through strong partnerships, inclusive values, and a passion for social impact, we aim to support young people in discovering their potential, strengthening their skills, and shaping a more connected, open, and engaged world.

  • We support youth in exploring new cultures through international mobility projects.
  • We encourage creativity and leadership through hands-on learning experiences.
  • We raise awareness on key social topics through workshops, campaigns, and events.
Our Projects

Our Projects

Discover how we turn ideas into impact through local and international projects that empower, educate, and connect young people.

Write It Down!

"Write It Down" was a training course held from March 11–19, 2025, aimed at equipping youth workers with practical skills to design and write successful Erasmus+ KA1 project applications.

Do It Yourself!

"Do It Yourself!" was an Erasmus+ training course organized by IYAC Bulgaria in March 2025, where participants developed and facilitated their own workshops, enhancing their skills in youth work and non-formal education.

Step Forward!

"Step Forward!" was an Erasmus+ youth exchange hosted by IYAC Bulgaria in January 2025 in Stara Zagora, where participants developed leadership, teamwork, and public speaking skills while exploring creative use cases of AI in building CVs and resumes to improve their career opportunities.

Keep Talking!

"Keep Talking!" was an Erasmus+ youth exchange focused on developing participants’ communication skills through Non-Violent Communication techniques and improvisational theater, promoting intercultural dialogue and active citizenship.
